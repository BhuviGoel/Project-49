Teddy Bear

Intersting Facts about a teddy bear:

  • A teddy bear is a stuffed toy in the form of a bear.
  • Named after President Theodore "Teddy" Roosevelt, the teddy bear became a popular children's toy
  • Teddy bears are among the most popular gifts for children and are often given to adults to signify love, affection, congratulations, or sympathy.

Chameleons

Intersting Facts about a chameleon:

  • Chameleons are highly specialized clade of Old World lizards with 202 species
  • Some chameleon species are able to change their skin colouration.
  • The chameleon was featured in Conrad Gessner's Historia animalium (1563), copied from De aquatilibus (1553) by Pierre Belon.

Pandas

Intersting Facts about a panda:

  • It is characterised by large, black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.
  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
